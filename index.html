About Us

A Legacy of Trust in Claims Adjustment

Founded in 1958, Benaco Adjusters has established itself as a pillar of excellence in the independent adjusting industry. For over six decades, we've maintained our commitment to impartiality, accuracy, and professionalism in every claim we handle.

Our longevity in this field is a testament to our unwavering dedication to ethical practices and our ability to adapt to the evolving landscape of insurance claims. We've built our reputation on the foundation of trust, serving both insurance carriers and policyholders with equal diligence.

At Benaco, we combine time-honored principles of fair adjustment with cutting-edge methodologies to deliver services that meet the highest industry standards. Our approach balances meticulous attention to detail with efficient claims processing.

What truly distinguishes us is our team of seasoned professionals who bring depth of experience across all categories of insurance claims, ensuring comprehensive solutions for even the most complex cases.

Our Services

Property Claims

Comprehensive assessment for residential and commercial property damage including fire, water, and natural disasters.

Auto Claims

Expert evaluation of vehicle damage, liability assessment, and fair settlement for all auto insurance claims.

Liability Claims

Thorough investigation and adjustment of third-party liability claims including bodily injury and property damage.

Workers' Compensation

Specialized handling of workers' compensation claims with focus on fair settlements and return-to-work programs.

Agricultural Claims

Expert assessment of crop and livestock insurance claims with specialized knowledge of agricultural risks.

Claims Consulting

Professional consulting services for complex claims, litigation support, and claims process optimization.
